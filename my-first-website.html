About Me

Basic Introduction

Hello, everyone! My name is Joel Alcaraz a.k.a. d-lar5. I graduated UC Davis with a Bachelor of Science in Psychology in March 2021. Soon after graduating, I started to feel that I wouldn't really put my degree to much use as I didn't feel passionate enough to pursue a career in Psychology. I picked up several hobbies and a part-time tutoring job in the meantime. One of those hobbies was learning how to code. I fell in love with it. Feeling stuck in my part-time job, I decided it was time to change my life forever. I am now taking all the steps I find necessary to become a Software Engineer.

Interests

Besides programming, I am also interested in playing chess and video games. I especially love playing Super Smash Bros. Ultimate (SSBU), which is the reason for my picture and my username. I attended Berkeley Chess School when I was a child and competed in various grade level and open tournaments. Ever since then, I developed a sense of competition that eventually led me to start competing in SSBU tournaments as well. Competition had also led me to enjoy watching NBA basketball, and man does it feel good to be a Warriors fan!

Goals

My ultimate goal right now is to become a Software Engineer. Of course, to accomplish that, I have to implement smaller goals. Steps that I am taking to reach that goal are watching chunks of Harvard's CS50 course everyday, practicing my programming skills by writing good code as much as I can, creating social media accounts dedicated to expanding my programming knowledge, and becoming a student of AppAcademy's coding bootcamp.
